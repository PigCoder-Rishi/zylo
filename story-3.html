“The Two-Minute Habit That Worked”

Action

I never thought two minutes could make a difference. But one day, I decided to try it. I took two minutes each day to check my home: lights left on, devices plugged in, thermostat settings.

The first week, it felt like I was wasting my time. The second week, it felt normal. The third week, I started to realize, it was super helpful!

The best part wasn't my electricity bill, though that was nice. I learned that you can take small actions, that add up to big changes.

Now I keep the habit small on purpose, only two minutes a day. And now that it’s easy, it’s sustainable.
