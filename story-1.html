“The Summer That Wouldn’t Cool Down”

Reflection

I used to love going for walks in the evening. But now the heat is still around when it gets dark.

And my kids are staying inside longer due to the humidity and my neighbors are missing their nice chats outside.

So I tried something: I started tracking the hour when the temperature cools down on my phone, the time I used to go on my walks. It’s not a scientific experiment, but it helps me think about climate change in a real way.
